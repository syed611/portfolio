About Me


I am a detail-oriented GIS Analyst, Data Analyst, and AutoCAD Designer with a strong background in Civil Engineering, site survey operations, and spatial data analysis. I started my professional journey with technical drafting and field-based survey work, where I gained hands-on experience in understanding ground conditions, drawings, and real-world project execution.

During my tenure at GeoVista Technologies Pvt. Ltd., I worked on large-scale infrastructure and telecom projects, most notably the BSNL BharatNet (ABP) Project. My responsibilities included monitoring Gram Panchayat–level survey data, verifying GPS accuracy, validating building availability and conditions, and converting raw field survey points into structured QGIS road and basemap layers.

I have extensive experience working with QGIS tools, AutoCAD drawings, Google Sheets, and Excel dashboards to manage, clean, and analyze large datasets. I regularly coordinated with field surveyors and supervisors to resolve data gaps, correct errors, and ensure timely and accurate reporting to project management teams.

I am known for my accuracy, discipline, problem-solving ability, and dedication. I am comfortable working under tight deadlines, handling real-time field data, and supporting multi-state projects across challenging terrains. My goal is to continue growing as a GIS & Data Analyst and contribute to impactful infrastructure and planning projects with precision and reliability.
